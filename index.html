~ about ~

Working professionally and independently for over 3 years in Denver I have had the opportunity to do live theatre, immersive theatre, film, commercial, and modeling. Compelled to work in the arts for the rest of my life, I try to find art that speaks to others. My biggest goal is to be honest in my work, and find others that feel the same. To create, and to work with other passionate artists is my dream, goal, and life.
